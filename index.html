Lin SUN

I am a lecturer at Soochow univerisity, Suzhou. My research interests include multi-core fiber signal processing, signal processing algorithm FPGA development and neural network equalization algorithm in optical communication, etc. Before that, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Hong Kong Polytechnic, where my research focused on short-range optical interconnection. Later, I joined the Transformative Project Team of Sun Yat-sen University (collaborator: Professor Zhaohui Li, National Jieqing).I received my doctoral degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019 and the title of "Outstanding Doctor of State Key Laboratory", and my bachelor of Engineering degree from Sichuan University in 2014.
